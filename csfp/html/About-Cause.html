Flood And Water Protection System

Flood and Water Protection System, or FAWPS, is an organization that aims to provide flood

victims with clean water resources in accordance with SDG 6, Clean Water and Sanitation.

It also provides ready and updated websites that alert people when a flood may come to their

respective areas. We strive to to make sure all flood victims and future flood victims are aware

and provided with clean sanitized water.
